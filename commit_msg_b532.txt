BUILD 532 fix: close-and-reopen overlapped handle for PDFWKRNL IOCTL timeout

PDFWKRNL driver only allows one handle (second CreateFileW returns err=5).
Replaced separate overlapped handle (g_hPdfwOverlapped) with close-and-reopen
strategy (TrySwitchToOverlapped): close sync handle, reopen with
FILE_FLAG_OVERLAPPED. On failure, restore sync handle (degraded but functional).

Updated 4 Init success paths and Shutdown to use new approach.
